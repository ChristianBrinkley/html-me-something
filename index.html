Superior Hiking Trail, Michigan

"In 2012 I drove with my scout troop up to lake Superior in Michigan and hiked 50 miles along the Superior Hiking Trail. We backpacked for 6 days hiking about 8 or 9 miles a day."

Continental Divide, Colorado

"In 2015 my scout troop took a trip to Colorado. During the trip we backpacked up to a mountain lake near the continental divide. We then took a day hike up to the top of a mountain on the continental divide. "

Berchtesgaden, Germany

"In 2016 my family visited Germany. During our trip we traveled to Berchtesgaden which is located in southern Germany in the Bavarian Alps. We hiked 18 miles total over 3 days. Instead of traditional backpacking we did hut to hut hiking where we stayed in "huts" which are cabins with bunks in them."

Chicago Basin, Colorado

"In 2017 my family took a trip to Colorado. Our backpacking trip started by riding the train between Silverton and Durango. We got off the train halfway between the two cities at the trailhead. We then hiked 5 miles up into the basin. The next day we hiked up to the lake in the bowl between the four mountains that make up the basin. On our final day we hiked out of the basin and caught the train on it's way to Silverton In the same place Where we got off."
